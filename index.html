Yoga & Meditation

Live Zen is a yoga and meditation school for anyone who is ready to relax, work hard, laugh or let go. We believe that yoga is for everyone regardless of age and body type. Whether you can touch your toes or not, Live Zen is here for helping you challenge your body and mind.


We are a school that offers Yoga and Meditation classes in most levels. Their formats tap into static/still postures with longer holds between each posture. This can provide the student an opportunity to move deeper into their internal reflection. Regardless of the format, our classes will provide you an experience that leaves you feeling energized and uplifted.


Our mission is to provide a genuine and sustainable yoga and meditqation practice that will continue to evolve the physical and emotional health of our students. We are proud of the individual attention that our students receive. Our instructors are passionate about teaching and are always willing to take that extra step to ensure that no student feels left behind.
